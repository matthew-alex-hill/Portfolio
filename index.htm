About Me
Hi there! I'm Matthew, a student of computer science and 3D artist. I love creating 3D scenes and atmospheres, whether for individual renders, animations or for a video game. I also create music and sound effects for my animations as well as some experimental computer programs. Have a look around this site to see all the work I've created!

I acheived 4 A*s at A level in Maths, Further Maths, Physics and Computing. I also acheived a first in my first year of studying Computing at Imperial College London, My highest scoring Modules were Programming (75%), Logic (79%) and Introduction to Databases (86%). I also acheived extra credit for a pass with distinction in Introduction to Philosophy. I discovered that this course was not right for me in my second year and am now looking for another placement where my individual creativity can be explored deeper.

My 3D modelling and animation skills in Blender have been developped via self teaching and online tutorials for around a year and my progression can be seen in this portfolio. I also am proficient in music creation softwares such as Sibelius, Audacity, Pro Tools and Fruit Loops. For me learning to use computers creatively has been really freeing as I love creating and constantly am thinking of ideas when in the shower or washing up, but have always had too clumsy hands to produce traditional art or to play instruments at a high level.
